Introduction

This privacy policy outlines how data is collected, used, and protected in our Vaccination app, developed for educational purposes as part of a doctor thesis.

Data Collection and Use

The app collects patient data, which is securely stored and used only for the purpose of facilitating communication between doctors and parents. Doctors can review patient progress and tick off doses, while parents can view marked doses and reminder days calculated from the calendar.
